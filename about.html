Our Story

Tawdi began with a simple mission: to combine the crunch of traditional Arabic sweets with the smooth luxury of global chocolate.

Founded in the heart of the desert, we use only the finest ingredients—from hand-picked pistachios to 100% pure cocoa butter. We believe that dessert is not just food; it is an experience of culture and craftsmanship.
